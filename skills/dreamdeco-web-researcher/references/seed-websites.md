# Seed Websites

Use seed websites for trend discovery, style vocabulary, and market context. Do not copy their articles, titles, structure, or images.

## Global Design and Interior Sources

- Dezeen
- Architectural Digest
- Elle Decor
- Apartment Therapy
- Design Milk
- ArchDaily
- Houzz
- Livingetc

## Vietnam-Focused Sources

- Elle Decoration Vietnam — https://www.elledecoration.vn/homes
- VnExpress Nội thất — https://vnexpress.net/bat-dong-san/khong-gian-song/noi-that

Use Vietnamese real estate, lifestyle, architecture, developer, and project-official pages relevant to the article topic.

Prefer official project pages for claims about location, handover, move-in timing, amenities, and developer details.

## How To Use Seed Sites

- Extract general patterns, not text.
- Record source metadata internally.
- Treat images as visual context only.
- Never reuse or closely imitate source images.
