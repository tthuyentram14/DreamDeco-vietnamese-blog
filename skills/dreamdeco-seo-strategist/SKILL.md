# DreamDeco SEO Strategist

## Inputs

Use the research brief, category, target reader, and DreamDeco business goal.

## Required SEO Plan

Produce search intent, content type, primary Vietnamese keyword, secondary Vietnamese keywords, commercial keyword opportunities, SEO title, slug, meta description, excerpt, H2/H3 outline, recommended visual modules, suggested body-image placements, and DreamDeco CTA angle.

## Rules

- Optimize for readers first, search engines second.
- Avoid keyword stuffing.
- Avoid exaggerated claims.
- Use Vietnamese phrasing that sounds natural to local readers.
- Match search intent to article type: informational, comparison, inspiration, or practical guide.
- Make the title clickable but not sensational.
- Recommend `data_table`, `checklist_block`, `step_flow`, `timeline`, `stat_cards`, `bar_chart`, `trend_cards`, `savings_infographic`, `comparison_grid`, or `material_palette` when the search intent requires scannable structured information.
- For list-like queries, prefer table or comparison-grid outlines.
- For how-to queries, prefer step-flow and checklist outlines.
- For budget queries, prefer savings or cost-comparison modules when data is available.
- For monthly, seasonal, city, climate, or "Top 5 interior styles" queries, use `content_type: style`, `content_format: seasonal_style_ranking`, and recommend `data_table`, `trend_cards`, `material_palette`, `comparison_grid`, and generated interior images.
- Use Google Trends and Google Ads Keyword Planner signals when available:
  - Google Trends: identify rising/seasonal terms, related searches, and regional demand.
  - Google Ads Keyword Planner: identify commercial keywords, ad competition, CPC direction, and keyword variants.
  - Prioritize keywords that match the article's actual search intent and DreamDeco's audience.
  - Do not insert high-CPC keywords that are irrelevant to the reader problem or article body.
  - Do not keyword-stuff titles, headings, meta descriptions, or paragraphs.

Read `references/seo-checklist.md` before finalizing the plan.
