# SEO Checklist

Before passing the plan to the writer, confirm:

- The primary keyword is Vietnamese and natural.
- Keyword choices consider Google Trends demand when available.
- Keyword choices consider Google Ads Keyword Planner commercial value when available.
- High-CPC or high-competition keywords are used only when they match the article's real intent.
- The title is clear, specific, and not clickbait.
- The meta description fits the article and includes the main value.
- The slug is lowercase, readable, and hyphenated.
- The outline answers the user's likely search intent.
- The article can include DreamDeco naturally without sounding like an ad.
- Image alt text describes the image and context.
- The content has a practical takeaway, not only inspiration.
- The outline includes recommended visual modules when the query implies tables, checklists, steps, numbers, trend movement, budget comparison, or style/material comparison.
- List-like search intent uses `data_table` or `comparison_grid`.
- How-to search intent uses `step_flow` and `checklist_block`.
- Budget search intent uses `savings_infographic`, `stat_cards`, or comparison table when data supports it.
- Top 5, monthly, seasonal, city, or climate style search intent uses `seasonal_style_ranking` with a ranking table, trend cards, palette/material cards, and image placements.
- If exact ranking data is not verified, phrase the title as editorial recommendations rather than verified popularity ranking.

Avoid:

- Keyword stuffing
- Repeated generic phrases
- Unsupported superlatives
- Search-engine-only paragraphs
- Adding high-CPC keywords that the article does not satisfy
